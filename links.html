Helpful Hiking Resources

Here are some useful websites to learn more about hiking, find trails, and get gear.

  • AllTrails — Find trail maps, reviews, and photos for hikes near you.
  • National Park Service — Official info about U.S. national parks, trails, and safety tips.
  • REI Co-op — Gear shop with helpful beginner guides and outdoor articles.
  • Hiking Project — Detailed GPS maps and trail info contributed by hikers.
  • Backpacker Magazine — Articles, skills, and trip ideas for hikers of all levels.
