Hiking Tips

Here are some simple tips to make your hikes safer and more enjoyable.

Essential Gear

  • Sturdy Hiking Boots: Protect your feet and give you good grip on rough terrain.
  • Water Bottle: Always carry enough water to stay hydrated.
  • Backpack: A small pack to hold snacks, gear, and first aid items.
  • Layers of Clothing: Weather changes fast, so bring a jacket or sweater.
  • Map or GPS: Even on easy trails, it’s smart to know where you are.

Safety Tips

  • Tell someone where you are going and when you’ll be back.
  • Check the weather before heading out.
  • Start with short, easy hikes if you’re a beginner.
  • Bring snacks for energy on longer hikes.
  • Stay on marked trails to avoid getting lost.

Nice-to-Have Extras

  • Trekking poles for stability.
  • Sunscreen and bug spray.
  • Camera or phone for photos.
  • Binoculars for spotting wildlife.
